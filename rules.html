How to Play ?

Find and "bust" the ghost hidden in the grid by strategically clicking on cells and using the color feedback as hints.

Colors Explanation

  • Red: The ghost is in this cell!
  • Orange: The ghost is 1-2 cells away.
  • Yellow: The ghost is 3-4 cells away.
  • Green: The ghost is 5 or more cells away.

Scores

- Each click costs you 1 point.
- Use the View Probabilities (Peep) button to reveal the likelihood of the ghost's location.
- When confident, click Bust the Ghost to guess the ghost's position.

You win if you find the ghost, but if your points or busts run out, it's Game Over!
